一、 as
    1. 作为，当作：
        She works as a director(导演).
    2. 像，如同：
        The bomb was disguiser(伪装) as a package(包).

二、 into
    1. 进入：
        I wandered(闲逛) into the hotel bao and order a drink.
    2. 转变成：
        Jemma had grown into a beautiful woman.
    3. 热衷于：
       She is into yoga(瑜伽).

三、 like
    1. 像：
        It's nothing like what happened in the mid-Seventies(七十年代中期).
    2. 像……样(人或事物的品质、特点)：
        What did she look like?
    3. 例如：
        It eats small animals like birds and mice.
    4. 像……一样：
        I was shaking(颤抖) all over, trembling(发抖的) like a leaf.

四、 through
    1. 穿过：
        The thief(小偷) got in through the window.
    2. 由于：
        He become ill through overwork(过度劳累).
    3. 从头到尾：
        He lay(躺着) awake(醒着) all through the night.
    
五、 after
    1. 在一段时间指挥：
        After the war(战争), I went back to work on the farm.
    2. 追逐，追求：
        The police are after him for burglary(盗窃).
    3. 由于：
        After he did to my faily, I hate(讨厌) him.
    4. 模仿：
        He takes after his father.

六、 over
    1. 在……上面(跨越)：
        Parry glanced at the clock over the door.
    2. 覆盖在……上：
        She put her hands over her ears.
    3. 在某地另一头(与地点名词连用)：
        Sandra's brother lives just over the rode fom our house.
    4. 超过：
        Over 50 percent of the company(公司)'s share are owner by the government(政府).
    5. 到处：
        Children were rushing around(..到处瞎跑) madly(疯狂地) all over the place
    6. 控制：
        The king has ruledover the people for ten year.
    7. 在……期间：
        Most hotel are fully(完全) booked(登记) over the holiday weekend.
    
七、 between
    1. 在……之间：
        They planted a lot of trees between the two bulidings.
    2. 私下：
        Between ourselves(我们自己), I know he wants to marry her.
    3. 来往于……之间：
        There is a path beteeen the house and the road.
    4. 由……分担：
        Between them they lended the big fish.
    5. 总共：
        The three sits employ(雇佣) 12500 people between them.

八、 against
    1. 反对：
        I'm against all forms of censorship.
    2. 违反：
        They forced(强迫) me to do things against my will.
    3. 逆着：
        We sailed against the wind.
    4. 靠：
        The seats have no back against which to lean.
    5. 以……为背景：
        The hat color looks good against your skin(皮肤).

九、 during
    1. 在……期间某一时刻：
        During his visit to South Africa, the president met Archbishop Tutu.
    2. 在……整个期间：
        During the summer season, all the hotels are full.

十、 without
    1. 缺乏：
        Robert likes to drink coffee without sugar(糖).
    2. 在否定词后，表强调(没有……则不能)：
        The old man cannot walk without a stick(拐杖).

十一、 within
    1. 在……之内：
        We expect an announcement(公告) within the next 24 hours.
    2. 在……范围内：
        Private(私人的) security(安全性) firms(公司) nust still operate(操作) within the limist of the law.
    
十二、 befoue
    1. 在……以前：
        I went for a run before breakfast.
    2. 在……之前：
        Quality(品质) comes before quantity(数量).

十三、 under
    1. 少于：
        The watch cannot be sold for under 100 yuan.
    2. 在……下面：
        What are your kids doing under the tabl?
    3. 处在……情况之下：
        The machine is still under warranty.
    4. 承受着：
        He kept the situation under control.
    5. 接受：
        Several players are under treatmentfor injuries.
    6. 依照：
        Under the terms of the agreement, our company will receive 40% of the profits.

十四、 around
    1. 环绕：
        The earth goes around the sun.
    2. 在……周围：
        The children sat around the teacher.
    3. 大概：
        The number of listeners tonight is around 100.
    4. 到处：
        They walked around the town looking for a place to eat.

十五、 among
    1. 在……中间：
        They strolled(散步) among the crowds.
    2. 属于：
        You are among the fortunate(幸运的).
    3.在……中(分配)；从……中(选择)：
        The cost(花费) should be shared(共享的) equally(平均的) among the three of you.

十六、 behind
    1. 在……之后：
        The teacher was standing behind me.
    2. 落后于：
        The project(计划) is already a month behind schedule.
    3. 支持：
        When it came to a crisis(危险), they all rallied(联合) behind their leader(领袖).
    4. 是……产生的原因:
        Police belive that a racist group is behind the attacks. (种族歧视是攻击产生的原因)

十七、 above
    1. 在……上面：
        The sun rose above the horizon0(地面).
    2. 超过：
        In most subject the students scored well above average.
    3. 不屑于：
        Jack was cheating(行骗)?I thought he was above that sort(种类) of thing(.4.(那种行为)).
    4. 尤其是：
        In their society(交往), honesty was prized(最有价值的) above all other virtues(美德).

十八、 below
    1. 在……下面：
        There is a supermarket below the department(机关部门) store.
    2. 少于：
        Duringthe night, temperatures dropped below zero.

二十、 toward
    1. 向：
        He ran toward his house.
    2. 对于：
        I have shown enough forbearance(节制) toward him.
    3. 接近：
        I'll phone you some time toward the end of the week.

二十一、 along
    1. 沿着：
        We walk along he road.
    2. 按照：
        Here are a few suggestions(民众意见箱) along this line.

二十二、 beyond
    1. 超越：
        Jenny won the competition beyond everyone's expectation(期待).
    2. 晚于：
        Don't stay here beyond midnight.
    3. 超过(数量，年龄)：
        Inflation(通货膨胀) had risen beyond 10%.
